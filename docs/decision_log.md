# Decision Log

## Purpose
Track important architectural, modeling, and controller decisions made during the project.

Each entry should capture:
- what was decided
- why it was decided
- what alternatives were considered
- what downstream impact it has

---

## Decision Template

### Decision ID
DEC-001

### Date
YYYY-MM-DD

### Topic
Short title of the decision.

### Context
Why this decision needed to be made.

### Options Considered
- Option A
- Option B
- Option C

### Decision
Chosen option.

### Rationale
Why this option was selected.

### Implications
What this changes in design, implementation, or testing.

### Follow-Up Actions
- Action 1
- Action 2
